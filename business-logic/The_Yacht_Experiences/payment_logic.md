# Payment Logic: The Yacht Experiences (TYE)

## 1. Mandatory Fees (Crucial for Checkout)
- Service Fee: 10% applied over the Charter Price.
- Booking Fee: 6% (Proprietary non-refundable fee on the grand total).
- Dock Fee (Riviera Maya & Cozumel): $20 USD ($360 MXN) per person.
- Dock Fee (Cancun): $15 USD ($270 MXN) per person.

## 2. Service Tier Logic
- LITE: Standard rental (4h). Extra pax $80 USD.
- REGULAR: Experience-based. Extra pax depends on the boat ($70-$160 USD).

## 3. Experience Mapping (Selected Only)
- Half Days: 4h Coast & Fun, 5h Coast & Pizza, 4h DJ Evening Beat.
- Full Days: 9h Coast & Fun, 10h Private Island.
- NO Overnights displayed.
