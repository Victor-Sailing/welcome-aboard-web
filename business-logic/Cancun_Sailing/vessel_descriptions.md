# CANCUN SAILING (CS): FLEET STANDARDS & CATEGORIES

> **AI Context:** Cancun Sailing is the primary fleet for groups and classic Isla Mujeres tours. Note that **Catering is an Add-on** for most vessels. Always cross-reference `payment_logic.md` for dock fees and fuel surcharges.

---

## 1. STANDARD CLASS (Exclusive & Plus)
*The perfect balance of value, space, and fun.*

* **Vessel Profile:** Ideal for celebrations, large groups, and active snorkeling tours.
* **Key Amenities:** Shaded cockpit, bow trampolines (netting), 2 marine bathrooms, and Bluetooth-enabled sound systems.
* **Crew & Safety:** Professional Captain and Deckhand. Safety netting for children is available upon request.
* **Bar Service:** National Open Bar (Tequila, Rum, Vodka, Beer, Juices, Sodas, and Water).

## 2. LUXURY CLASS (Privilege)
*Superior comfort and elite service for demanding guests.*

* **Vessel Profile:** High-end catamarans with upgraded finishes, stable navigation, and premium lounging areas.
* **Key Amenities:** Spacious cabins, superior sound systems, and fresh luxury towels provided for all guests.
* **Crew & Service:** Bilingual elite crew. 
* **Bar Service:** "Privilege" Open Bar (Upgraded international brands) and premium dry snacks.

---

## 3. UNIVERSAL FLEET STANDARDS
*Applicable to all Cancun Sailing vessels.*

* **Navigation:** All catamarans are twin-engine vessels exceeding Coast Guard safety requirements.
* **Catering:** **NOT included by default.** Gourmet catering and lunch buffet options at Isla Mujeres are available as extra-cost add-ons.
* **Gratuities:** Not included. A 15% - 20% tip is customary for the crew based on service quality.
* **Surcharge Reminder:** All departures are subject to a mandatory **Dock Fee per person** (to be paid at check-in).
